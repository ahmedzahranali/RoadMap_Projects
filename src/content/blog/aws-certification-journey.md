I was able to acquire  **AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C03)** and **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner** certifications this month!

I was able to achieve this after finishing stephane maarek's SAA course on udemy

### Bridging Theory and Practice

I finished the course and immediatley started taking the practice exams provided by stephane maarek and toturial dojo, after some practice i gained the confidence to take the official exam.

### Why the Solutions Architect Track?

This track for me was the best first step in my cloud journey and I'm just getting started and the next step for my is to get the certified developer certificate.
